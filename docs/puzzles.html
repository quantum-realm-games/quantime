Welcome to the Quantum Realm! The word Quantum might seem strange and scary, but it doesn't have to be. It's a label for the rules that describe all behavior in the universe, so far as humanity knows.

Often, the conversation about quantum rules starts with a lot of imagination and mathematics.

Learn more about the project

Today, you're going to make a new friend: Sph1r0 (pronounced s-f-ee-ro). Sph1r0 likes to talk about big ideas by using everyday objects.

He's going to show you a chess board. He's going to show you how the pieces on the board move. Then he's going to show you how you can think about the rules of quantum behavior as a game of chess.
