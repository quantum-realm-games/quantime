About

The resources on this site are produced in a collaboration between Caltech, Google, Quantum Realm Games , and many more people and academic partners.

Our goal is to help the public develop an intuition about the principles of quantum physics. We believe that those principles are best introduced through play.

We would love to hear your feedback, answer your questions, or work with you: get in touch with us by using the form below.

For more activities and information about quantum physics, see the Quantime page at the Q12 Education Partnership website.
